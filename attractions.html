Glasgow Botanic Gardens

Glasgow Botanic Gardens, located in the West End of Glasgow and managed by Glasgow City Council is arguably the finest Garden in Glasgow, and is dominated by the recently restored Kibble Palace.

The Kibble Palace. a magnificent Curvilinular Glasshouse designed by John Kibble (full details elsewhere in this site) houses the National collection of tree ferns.

Kelvingrove Art Gallery and Museum

Kelvingrove Museum opened in 1901 and is a firm favourite with local people and visitors. It has stunning architecture and a family friendly atmosphere.

Explore our 22 galleries and discover everything from art to animals, Ancient Egypt to Charles Rennie Mackintosh and so much more. We also have a changing programme of temporary exhibitions and displays.
